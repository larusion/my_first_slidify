Olympic Track Times from 1896 to 1964

This app displays the olympic track times in tenths of a second versus over the years that they were recorded, i.e., from 1896 to 1964.

This dataset comes from Package 'cluster.datasets' of CRAN.

References:

Hartigan, J. A. (1975). Clustering Algorithms, John Wiley, New York.

What a user needs to do

The user needs to choose the meters that the athletes ran.

The options are:

  1. 100 m
  2. 200 m
  3. 400 m
  4. 800 m
  5. 1500 m
  6. 5000 m
  7. 10000 m
